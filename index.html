THE FOUNDER

The story behind the company, and what's to come...

Peter Leonard

CEO & Founder

Our founder has background in the arts, science, and tech - he holds a BA in Integrative Arts from Penn State, and came to coding during his graduate studies at Tulane in the field of Music, Science and Technology. His early work in special education informs his deepest passions in the tech world - to create apps and software that use sound to help those with neurological issues. Sound Applications is currently in the R & D phase for their first med-tech app, Sound for Aging, aimed for release in 2025.
